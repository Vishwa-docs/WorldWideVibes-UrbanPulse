## 1. OFFICIAL CHALLENGE STATEMENT

**Source:** [Challenge Brief (Confluence)](https://genaijira.atlassian.net/wiki/external/MzQ4ZWU0ZjE0NmQzNDk3NzkyZjM4NDcxOTJkM2YzZDg)

> Build a vibe-coded solution that addresses one of the key challenge areas below using data from the City of Montgomery Data Portal and publicly available web data powered by Bright Data.

### Four Challenge Tracks:
1. **Civic Access & Community Communication**
2. **Workforce, Business & Economic Growth** ← *Our track*
3. **Smart Cities, Infrastructure & Public Spaces**
4. **Public Safety, Emergency Response & City Analytics**

### Suggested Problem Areas (from challenge brief):
- Improve city communication and public access
- **Train and reskill the workforce**
- Innovate classroom learning and student support
- Analyze patterns in public transportation usage
- Compare crime statistics with public perception and messaging
- Analyze and improve insights from 911 data
- **Compare business licenses with foot traffic trends**
- **Identify and address vacant properties**
- Track and optimize city construction projects and costs
- Analyze and improve usage of city-owned properties

### Suggested Tools (NONE mandatory):
- Google AI Studio: https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/docs/ai-studio-quickstart
- Montgomery Open Data Portal: https://opendata.montgomeryal.gov/
- Bright Data: https://docs.brightdata.com/scraping-automation/crawl-api/quick-start
- Google Stitch (optional)

## 9. ALIGNMENT ANALYSIS — OUR PROJECT (UrbanPulse)

### What We Have:
- ✅ Site-selection intelligence platform for **Montgomery, AL**
- ✅ Real data from **Google Places, Census API, Bright Data**
- ✅ AI-powered analysis via **Azure OpenAI (GPT-4o)**
- ✅ Walk scores, demographics, market gap analysis, investment analysis
- ✅ Site reports with AI narrative
- ✅ Interactive map with property scoring
- ✅ Python/FastAPI backend + React/TypeScript frontend

### Alignment Strengths:
| Criteria | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Challenge alignment | ✅ Strong | Workforce & Economic Growth — site selection drives business creation |
| Montgomery-specific | ✅ Strong | All data centered on Montgomery, AL |
| Real data (not mock) | ✅ Strong | Google Places, Census API, live endpoints |
| Working prototype | ✅ Strong | Full-stack app, 104 tests passing |
| AI-powered | ✅ Strong | Azure OpenAI for narratives, analysis, reports |
| Bright Data | ✅ Used | +3 bonus points |
| Social value | ✅ Moderate | Helps identify underserved areas, vacant properties |
| Originality | ⚠️ Moderate | Site selection tools exist, but civic-focused angle is unique |
| Commercialization | ⚠️ Moderate | Could be pitched as SaaS for city planners/developers |

### Gap Analysis — What Could Be Stronger:

1. **Business license data integration** — Challenge brief specifically mentions "compare business licenses with foot traffic trends." We could integrate Montgomery business license data from ArcGIS.

2. **Vacant property identification** — Challenge brief mentions "identify and address vacant properties." We have vacancy rates from Census but could show actual vacant properties on map.

3. **Workforce reskilling angle** — Our track is "Workforce, Business & Economic Growth" but our tool focuses more on business/site selection. Adding workforce data (unemployment, job training programs, skills gaps) would strengthen the workforce dimension.

4. **Montgomery-specific open data** — We use Census and Google Places but could also pull from Montgomery's ArcGIS portal directly (311 requests, most visited locations, visitor origin data) to show deeper integration with city data.

5. **Demo/presentation video** — Required for submission. Need to create this.

6. **Problem statement clarity** — Need a clear written explanation tying our tool to the specific challenge of economic growth in Montgomery.
